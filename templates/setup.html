To ensure your touchscreen is recognized, press the following button then tap your e-reader's screen within five seconds.

To setup page turning, press each button below then perform the specified input on your e-reader within five seconds.

If you require support for both portrait and landscape, tap the screen in an area that will trigger the correct page turn in both orientations (e.g. near the top right and bottom left while in portrait).

Otherwise, using a swipe is recommended. A tap might occasionally trigger a footnote or hyperlink, and on some older models it can be hard to record a tap that will reliably turn pages.

If you make a mistake or ever need to change a recording, just re-record the input and it will overwrite the previous one.

Note: if your e-reader automatically rotates the screen orientation, when it's upside down the page turns will be reversed.

Kobo e-readers turn their Wi-Fi off after a short period to conserve battery life. To prevent this, enable the developer setting 'ForceWifiOn'.
